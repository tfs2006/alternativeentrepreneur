ChatGPT Cheat Sheet for Affiliate Marketers

If you landed on an older ChatGPT cheat sheet link, this is the updated version. Use it as a fast reference for researching keywords, outlining posts, comparing tools, and tightening calls to action without turning your content into generic AI sludge.

This page replaces an older resource URL. I kept the best parts and trimmed it down to the prompt patterns that are actually useful in a modern affiliate content workflow.

The 4 prompt patterns that matter most

Search intent check

Ask ChatGPT to classify whether a keyword is informational, commercial, or comparison-driven before you write anything.

Outline builder

Generate a first-pass structure, then rewrite it based on the exact outcome you want the page to produce.

Angle expansion

Use it to create alternate hooks, objections, and comparison angles instead of publishing the first obvious take.

Internal linking map

Feed it your site sections and ask for context-relevant internal link opportunities before publishing.

A practical starter formula

  • Give ChatGPT the keyword, audience, monetization goal, and page type.
  • Ask for the 3 strongest search intents behind the query.
  • Request a clean outline with sections, objections, and CTA moments.
  • Have it suggest internal links to related guides and tools.
  • Rewrite the draft in your own voice before publishing.
