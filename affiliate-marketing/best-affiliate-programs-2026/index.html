Best Affiliate Programs 2026 (Ranked by CPA)

Not all affiliate programs are worth your time. These are the ones with high commission rates, proven conversion rates, and real earning longevity — ranked by what actually matters.

What Makes an Affiliate Program Worth Joining

I filter affiliate programs by three criteria before joining: commission rate (is the payout meaningful per sale?), cookie duration (does a 24-hour cookie kill your conversions?), and recurring vs one-time (does one referral keep paying monthly?). The programs below score well on all three.

Where to Find More Programs

These networks host hundreds of programs you can browse and apply to in one place:

  • PartnerStack — Best for B2B SaaS programs
  • Impact — Best for established consumer and business brands
  • ShareASale — Broad mix including physical and digital products
  • CJ Affiliate — Large network, includes many recognizable brands

Programs to Avoid

Amazon Associates for competitive niches: 3% on most categories, 24-hour cookie window, and rates cut without notice. Fine for supplemental income if you already have Amazon-focused content, but don't build your entire business around it.

Any "lifetime 50% commission" with no verifiable track record — these programs often disappear or change terms after you've sent significant traffic.
